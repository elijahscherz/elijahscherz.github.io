Code Development

It's 2018 and this is my sixth year playing friz. Over the last couple years I've competed in multiple tournaments including the U.S. Open in Minnesota as well as Club Regional Championships. I love the sport, I love the people.

Music

From a young age, I grew up in a musical household. My parents both play various instruments as well as sing, and those skills have passed down to me. I play guitar and sing, and can carry a beat on drums and play simple chord songs on piano. Can't seem to forget how much of a wonder music is, and that is likely what brings me to listen to so many tunes. I've got pretty wide tastes, from alternative, to classic rock, to punk pop. Take a look at my Spotify to find some new bands and some great playlists.
